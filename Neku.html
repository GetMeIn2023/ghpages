ABOUT US

Step into a world of purrs, whiskers, and delightful coffee aromas at Nekuppi Cafe, the premier cat cafe that blends the best of both worlds - the love of cats and the pleasure of sipping on your favorite coffee concoctions. Our cat cafe is a safe and loving environment, providing a forever home for these beautiful felines while creating a serene space for you to unwind and destress. Whether you're a seasoned cat enthusiast or simply seeking solace amidst the bustling city life, Nekuppi Cafe offers an oasis of tranquility and joy.
